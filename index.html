Services

I offer a range of beginner-friendly services based on what I’ve learned in school and through hands-on projects. I continue to improve these skills as I work toward becoming a better developer and designer.

Web Development

I build simple and functional websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. As a student, I am continuously improving my coding skills through classwork, tutorials, and personal projects.

Responsive Design

I create layouts that adjust smoothly across phones, tablets, and desktops. I practice modern responsive techniques like Flexbox and Grid to ensure websites look good on every device.

Creative Design

I enjoy designing clean, minimal, and visually appealing interfaces. I experiment with tools like Figma and Canva to bring ideas to life and improve my design sense.

UI/UX Basics

I study the fundamentals of user experience and interface design, including wireframing, user flow, and basic prototyping. I aim to make designs both beautiful and easy to use.

School & Personal Projects

I work on various coding and design projects for school and self-learning. These projects help me practice new concepts and build a stronger portfolio.

Problem-Solving & Debugging

I’m learning how to identify issues in code and fix them using logical thinking and research. Debugging has helped me strengthen my understanding of programming concepts.
